<Steps>
    1. Reboot your computer and try to run Multi Theft Auto again.

        By rebooting your system, you will effectively remove all traces of Cheat Engine
        running on your system.

    2. If that didn't work, uninstall *Cheat Engine* from your computer, and reboot it.

    3. Try to run Multi Theft Auto again.
</Steps>
